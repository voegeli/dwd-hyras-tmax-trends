# DWD HYRAS TASMAX Results

Dataset: DWD HYRAS TASMAX v6-1, complete years 1951-2025. 2026 is excluded because it is incomplete as of 2026-06-12.

Hot-day count definition: annual count of days where at least one valid Germany grid cell reached the threshold. Area-share metrics use the share of valid grid cells on each day.
